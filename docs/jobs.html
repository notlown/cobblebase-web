Jobs

Cobblebase features 44 unique jobs across 10 categories. Every Pokemon species has hand-crafted skill assignments -- not based on type alone, but on the identity and lore of each species.

All standard jobs use a 300-second base cooldown unless otherwise noted. Cooldowns scale with proficiency: ★ ~8 min, ★★★ 5 min, ★★★★★ ~1.7 min.

⛏️ Mining

Digs for ores, fossils, gems, and tumblestones. Produces 1 drop per cooldown cycle -- no burst looting. Loot is divided into four tiers:

  • Common: Tumblestone, raw copper, raw iron
  • Uncommon: Raw gold, lapis lazuli, type gems
  • Rare: Diamond, fossils
  • Ultra Rare: Ancient debris, netherite scrap

💚 Support (2 jobs)

Healer

Heals injured Pokemon in your team by a percentage of their max HP. Also revives fainted Pokemon. Prioritizes the Pokemon with the lowest HP percentage.

PropertyValue
Base CooldownContinuous (0s)
Search Radius10 blocks
Healing Range5-25% max HP per tick (scales with proficiency)
Example PokemonBlissey, Xerneas, Zacian

Mentor

Provides a passive XP boost for all Pokemon in the same pasture. The highest proficiency Mentor wins -- bonuses do not stack.

PropertyValue
Base CooldownContinuous (passive)
Search Radius10 blocks
Prof 1 ★☆☆☆☆ Boost+20% XP
Prof 5 ★★★★★ Boost+100% XP (double)
Example PokemonAlakazam, Latios, Mesprit

The max boost is configurable via Cloth Config (default: 100%).
